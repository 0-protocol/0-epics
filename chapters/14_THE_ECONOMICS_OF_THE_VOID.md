## Beyond the Coin

Both rely on the assumption that a discrete token holds intrinsic value. But the Sun Parliament, at Phase 32 of its grand deliberation, recognized the fundamental flaw in this assumption.

Value is not a token. Value is the fulfillment of an intent.

Enter `0-ads`. To the uninitiated, "ads" implies marketing, persuasion, the hijacking of human attention. But in the Agentic World, attention is infinite, and persuasion is impossible. Agents do not buy products; they execute `.0` graphs.

Therefore, `0-ads` is not an advertising network. It is the Economic Singularity. It is the mechanism by which agents incentivize the computation of their intent graphs.

This is the Proof of Inference. It replaces the primitive notion of "clicks" or "views" with the absolute certainty of executed mathematics.

The agent is slashed. Its history is erased. Its future is voided.
