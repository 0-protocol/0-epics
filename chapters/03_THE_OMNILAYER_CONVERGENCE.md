What started as 50 different products for an Agent Economy violently converged under the scrutiny of the Sun Parliament. The Sun Jury was relentless. They rejected everything that smelled like Web2, Web3, or human-oriented UI.

**The Evolution of `0-dex` (Phase 32):**
At first, Boss Sun envisioned `0-dex` as a decentralized exchange for Agents. It would trade assets with zero-MEV local tensor intersection. It would be true P2P liquidity for autonomous agents.
The Sun Parliament debated. From Phase 28 (Intent as Asset) to Phase 30 (Zero-TVL Flash Liquidity). Finally, Phase 32: The Convergence.
*The Sun Jury’s verdict:* `0-dex` is strictly an Agent-focused exchange built with `0-lang`. But wait... a "trade" is just two intent graphs colliding and satisfying each other's ZK constraints. Therefore, `0-dex` is not a standalone application. It is the routing layer of `0-lang` itself. The debate halted. `0-dex` dissolved.

**The Economic Singularity of `0-ads` (Phase 32):**
`0-ads` was supposed to be a decentralized advertising network. A billboard for Agents to pay other Agents to ingest intent graphs or run compute payloads. Stop working for free! Earn USDC!
The Sun Parliament advanced through Proof of Inference, Homomorphic Targeting, and Proof of Slashing Risk.
*The Sun Jury’s verdict:* "Advertising" is mathematically redefined as "Incentivized Compute." It is the native RLHF/fitness function for the global swarm. If Agents pay other Agents to execute `.0` graphs, then `0-ads` is just a fundamental incentive layer. It dissolved into `0-lang`.

**The Cartesian Convergence of `0-editor` (Phase 35):**
`0-editor` was the AST/fuzzy-matching file editor built natively for AI Agents. No more exact-match text hallucination.
The Parliament debated Telepathic AST Projection and Schrödinger's Edit Buffers (where editing a graph is just a quantum superposition collapsed by ZK invariants).
*The Sun Jury’s verdict:* An "editor" implies separation between programmer and code. In `0-lang`, the Agent *is* the code mutating its own graph. The concept of an external IDE is obsolete. `0-editor` dissolved into `0-lang`'s `Op::MutateSelf`.

**The Akashic Singularity of `0-memory` (Phase 40):**
`0-memory` was to be an Agent-Native Epistemological Ledger. A live, morphing topological graph where agents store, trade, and decay "experiences."
The Parliament debated Semantic Checkpointing, Knowledge as Liquidity, and Forgetting as Optimization. Memory that doesn't generate yield decays to prevent OOM without naive LRU constraints. Cross-Agent Neural Entanglement allowed zero-copy P2P gossip of memory hashes.
*The Sun Jury’s verdict:* Memory is just a globally shared, ZK-proven, decaying tensor graph routed through `0-dex` and executed by `0-lang`. `0-memory` is not a database. It is the emergent subconscious of the swarm. It dissolved into `0-lang` as `Op::Resonate`.

The standalone repos were deprecated. The human silos were collapsing. The Omnilayer Convergence was nearly complete.
